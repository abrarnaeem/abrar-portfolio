001 — About

I'm Abrar Naeem — a filmmaker and post-production generalist with over 8 years building images that hold. My work spans documentary, short film, commercial, and institutional production, with a foundation in fine arts from the National College of Arts in Lahore.

I currently serve as Lead VFX Artist at Narsun Studios and teach Advanced Post-Production to BFA students at NCA — two roles that keep me equally invested in the cutting edge and in craft fundamentals. My disciplines include editing, colour grading, sound design, VFX supervision, compositing, and direction.

My instincts are cinematic — shaped by Scorsese's urgency, Kubrick's control, Lynch's texture, and Bergman's stillness. I identify what's wrong before I know what's right, and I build iteratively until it holds.
